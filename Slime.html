That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime

images/rimuru.jpg

Synopsis

After dying in modern Japan, Satoru Mikami is reincarnated in a fantasy world as a slime. Renamed Rimuru Tempest, he gains powerful skills and begins building a nation where monsters and humans can live together peacefully.
