Mushoku Tensei

images/rudeus.jpg

Synopsis

A jobless man is reincarnated into a magical world as Rudeus Greyrat. Determined to live a better life, he trains in magic and embarks on adventures across the world.

Main Characters

  • Rudeus Greyrat
  • Eris Boreas Greyrat
  • Sylphiette
  • Roxy Migurdia
  • Orsted

Story Arcs

  1. Childhood Arc
  2. Demon Continent Arc
  3. Magic University Arc
  4. Labyrinth Arc
  5. Final Battle Arc

Power System

Magic is divided into several schools and ranks, ranging from Beginner to God-ranked abilities.

Character Role

Fun Fact

Mushoku Tensei is widely credited with helping popularize the modern isekai genre.
