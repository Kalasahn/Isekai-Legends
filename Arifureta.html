Arifureta: From Commonplace to World's Strongest

/hajime.jpg" alt="Hajime Nagumo from Arifureta" width="300">

Synopsis

Arifureta follows Hajime Nagumo, an ordinary high school student who is transported to another world with his classmates. After being betrayed and left for dead inside a dangerous dungeon, Hajime must fight to survive and become strong enough to return home.

Main Characters

  • Hajime Nagumo
  • Yue
  • Shea Haulia
  • Tio Klarus
  • Kaori Shirasaki

Story Arcs

  1. Orcus Labyrinth Arc
  2. Great Reisen Labyrinth Arc
  3. Gruen Volcano Arc
  4. Divine Mountain Arc
  5. Final Battle Arc

Power System

Characters gain abilities through classes, levels, skills, ancient magic and powerful equipment. Hajime becomes one of the strongest characters after surviving the Great Orcus Labyrinth.
