Overlord

images/ainz.jpg

Synopsis

Momonga, a powerful guild master, finds himself trapped inside the virtual world of Yggdrasil after the game's servers shut down. Taking the name Ainz Ooal Gown, he begins conquering the new world while seeking answers about his situation.

Power System

The series uses RPG-style levels, classes, magic tiers, racial abilities and special skills inherited from Yggdrasil.
